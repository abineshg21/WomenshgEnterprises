Our Vision

Women SHG Enterprises is a startup envisaged to act as resource center where all necessary support is made available from a single platform for providing Training, Capacity Building in various technologies providing livelihood and other Business opportunities and improving the quality of life of the women by reducing their drudgery, Better Resource management through value addition etc.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to building an inclusive platform that empowers women in self-help groups by providing opportunities to connect, learn, and grow their businesses. Through collaboration, skill development, and access to resources, we aim to foster financial independence and sustainable growth, enabling women entrepreneurs to thrive in their communities.
